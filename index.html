A little bit about myself

Before coming to the United States six years ago, I wanted to study accounting because I like math, so I thought the only thing I could do with math was accounting. But, after taking some Web Development classes in high school, I realized that I could combine math with technology, so I decided to study Computer Science.

Now that I'm in my third year of college, I want to continue this path and learn new things about this field. Last summer, I completed my first Internship and, I hope I can get more opportunities like this in the future.

Skills

My journey in computer science started in simple HTML and CSS back in high school. Then, I tried simple JavaScript a year after. In my first semester of college we used basic Python, but I didn't dive into it deeper.

My next step is to learn more Web Development technologies, like node.js, React, Bootstrap, etc.
